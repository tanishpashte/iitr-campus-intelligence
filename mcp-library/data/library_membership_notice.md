## 1. Membership Activation & Verification

* **Account Creation:** Library memberships for all first-year PhD and PG students are pre-created[cite: 11]. 
* **Membership Number:** Your Institute Enrolment Number serves as your unique library membership number[cite: 11].
* **Mandatory Physical Verification:** Before books can be borrowed, students must visit the library issue/return counter in person for account details verification[cite: 11].
* **Borrowing Process:** Once verified, books from the first-floor collection can be issued by producing an Institute ID Card or Medical Booklet at the counter[cite: 11].

---

## 2. Text Book Loan Scheme (TBLS) Registration

The TBLS facility under this specific notice carries distinct eligibility criteria and payment workflows[cite: 11]:

* **Eligibility:** This specific PG TBLS facility is open *only* to students enrolled in **all M.Sc. programs** and **M.Tech. Earth Science** courses[cite: 11].
* **One-Time Registration Fee:** ₹200/-[cite: 11].
* **Exemptions:** Students belonging to the SC/ST social categories are entirely exempted from this fee[cite: 11].
* **Payment Method:** Advance payments can be completed via the provided QR code or the institutional UPI handle[cite: 11]:
  * **UPI ID:** `libraryiitr@sbi`[cite: 11]
* **Registration Requirement:** The physical or digital payment receipt must be presented at the library counter during TBLS registration[cite: 11].

## 3. Library OPAC Portal Login Instructions

Students can access their online library profile portal by following these steps[cite: 11]:

1. Visit the primary library portal: [https://mgcl.iitr.ac.in](https://mgcl.iitr.ac.in)[cite: 11].
2. Click on the **"My Account @ MG Central Library"** link[cite: 11].
3. Log in using the following credential architecture[cite: 11]:
   * **User ID:** Your Enrolment Number[cite: 11].
   * **Password Pattern:** `FIRST FOUR LETTERS OF YOUR NAME (IN CAPS)@Last four digits of your mobile number` (as registered with the Academic Section)[cite: 11].

> ### 💡 Password Example
> * **Student Name:** Sanjay Kumar[cite: 11]
> * **Registered Mobile:** 1234567890[cite: 11]
> * **Generated Password:** `SANJ@7890`[cite: 11]

### Critical Security Actions
* **Immediate Password Update:** Users are required to modify their default password immediately after their initial login[cite: 11].
* **Data Verification:** Review your profile data (Name, email, mobile number, etc.)[cite: 11]. If an error is detected, updates must be handled physically at the issue/return counter. **Online requests for library profile data modifications will not be entertained.**[cite: 11]
