# Services

## Membership

IITR faculty, students, and staff are bona-fide members of the library. They have to get their membership activated by producing documentary proof of their Employee No. / Enrolment No. at the library circulation counter as per schedule (for students only).

The MGCL also offers membership to IIT Roorkee Alumni, IITR retired faculty, officers, staff, and other professionals on payment of fees as per the details below:

## Wi-Fi Connectivity @MGCL

All floors in the library are Wi-Fi enabled zones. User may use their laptops, mobile, or any other similar devices for accessing catalogue and academic information. 

## Reprographic/ Printing @MGC
- Reprography and computer printing facility is open for all the users from 10.30 A.M. to 05.30 P.M. (Lunch- 01.00 PM -02.00 pm) on all working days at a flat rate of Re. 2/- per page (B&W) and 5/- per page (Coloured). Only electronic payment is acceptable.
- Request for reprography of a document should be made on a prescribed form available with library staff.
- The Library strictly undertakes to reproduce material required for personal research and study purposes only.
- Photocopies of the unpublished dissertation/ thesis shall not be provided unless permission of the author is obtained.
- All responsibilities for questions pertaining to copyright that may arise due to violation of Copyright Act and any misuse made of copies shall strictly lie with person making the work order request.

## Payment Through POS
- Users can pay their dues through QR code/UPI.
- No payment will be accepted in cash. Borrowers can pay their dues on or before the registration for next year/leaving the Institute.

## Inter Library Loan
Under the Inter Library Loan Scheme, Research Paper/Article not available in our library collection is requested from neighborhood libraries or sister institutions on Inter Library Loan for meeting the special needs of all the potential readers. Requisition for Inter Library Loan of material should be send with the recommendation of the Head of Departments or the guides of research scholars.The same can also be sent through email to library@iitr.ac.in 

## Text Book Loan Scheme
The Text Book Loan Scheme section exclusively contains 62,000(approx.) editions of text books prescribed for study at the Undergraduate and all M.Sc/M.Tech (Earth Sci. & Bio tech.) level.A student may become member of this scheme on payment of prescribed onetime fee Rs 250/- (except SC/ST candidates). One may get three text books issued for semester duration. Text books may be exchanged on all working days. A fine of RS. 2.00 per volume per day shall be charged after the last day of return is over.

* Students may get 2 more books issued after TBLS Schedule.

Registration:
Only Privileged students can register for TBLS and become the member of the scheme on payment of prescribed onetime fee Rs 250/- (except SC/ST candidates). Every year before commencing the odd semester, the registration is done on the scheduled date. Student has to produce the admission slip and caste certificate (for SC/ST candidates) at the time of registration. 

## Cyber Zone/E-Resource Terminals
MG Central Library provides sufficient number of latest computers for browsing academic information available online through Internet. Separate computers have also been provided at every floor for searching library catalogue. 

## SMS/E-mail Alert Service
Through SMS/E-mail Alert service, The MGCL users receive the details of every transcation made in their account in the library, also the overdue reminders/ notices are being sent to the user in due course of time through this services.
